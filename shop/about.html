ABOUT US

Mikey's cuts is Kenya's fastest growing barber shop with several brances in Nairobi specializing in Commercial hair cut and special Residential haircut services on request. Our vision is to build a company that offers much more than just haircuts to clients. We are well known for our exceptional haircuts, massage and great customer experience. We provide only the highest quality haircuts. Our brand is about dramatically enhancing our clients' appearance, experience and satisfaction.
